Peregrine

Peregrine is a desktop app allowing users to manage their health record and communicate with their medical team. Peregrine also allows users to build and save exercise routines, mindfulness practices, and personalized goals. The app is designed for people looking to more closely track their medical data and incorporate exercise/mindfulness into their overall health and wellness.

Discord Design Community

As a career changer and UX boot camp graduate, I know the frustration of designing in isolation. Having an active community of fellow students and design enthusiasts takes the experience of learning design from laborious and lonely to exciting and rewarding. Accidental Solutions is a server built with the idea of creating space for design students to learn and grow together. The server encourages active discussion and collaboration between students.
