Peregrine

00 Project Overview

Tools

  • Figma
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe Illustrator

Role

  • UX designer

Peregrine is a desktop app allowing users to manage their health record and communicate with their medical team. Peregrine also allows users to build and save exercise routines, mindfulness practices, and personalized goals. The app is designed for people looking to more closely track their medical data and incorporate exercise/mindfulness into their overall health and wellness.

01 Exploring the Problem

Problem Statement

Users need a fast, confidential and efficient means to access, record, review, update and search their health history and fitness/wellness habits and routines because their time is stretched thin from other responsibilities. 02 Building an Understanding of the User

Meet Brooke

Needs:

  1. A workout tracker and goal setting features that facilitate her conditioning prior to her upcoming half marathon.
  2. A place to find new exercise ideas for herself and her clients.
  3. A medical record interface that both she and her doctor can manage.
  4. A food diary / nutrition tracker to facilitate healthy diet choices for managing her type I diabetes
